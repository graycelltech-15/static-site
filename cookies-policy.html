Cookies Policy

Effective date: August 28, 2025

This policy explains how Momentum Group uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that your web browser saves on your device when you visit a site. They help the site work properly and remember things about your visit.

A cookie usually contains the website name, a small ID number, and a time limit for how long it stays on your device. It does not run programs, and it cannot look at files on your computer.

What we use cookies for

We use cookies for these purposes:

  • 1. Essential operation and security. These are needed for the site to work.
  • 2. Preferences. These remember choices you make, such as language.
  • 3. Analytics. These help us understand traffic and improve the site.

We do not use cookies to show targeted ads.

What information cookies collect

Cookies can collect data like your device type, browser type, pages viewed, time on page, and general location based on IP address. Some of this may be personal information when it can identify or be linked to you.

Consent

Where the law requires consent for non-essential cookies, we will ask for it through a banner or settings on the site. You can withdraw consent at any time by changing your cookie settings on the site if available or by clearing or blocking cookies in your browser.

How to control cookies

You can:

  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Use our cookie settings, if shown on the site, to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

If you block cookies, parts of the site may not work properly.

Third parties and location of processing

Some cookies are set by our service providers to provide analytics or site features. These providers may process information in other countries. We take steps to require appropriate protection for information handled by our providers.

How long cookies last

Some cookies last only while your browser is open. Others stay on your device for a longer period and then expire automatically. You can remove them at any time through your browser settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our use of cookies or to the law. The effective date will show the latest version.
